Class: Coins

Coins()

Represents a collectible coin in the game. Coins are animated and positioned randomly within specified bounds. Extends the MovableObject class.

Constructor

new Coins()

Initializes a new coin, sets its position randomly within specified bounds, and starts the coin animation.
