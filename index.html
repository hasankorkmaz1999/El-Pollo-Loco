How To Play

Here are the key bindings for the game:

  • Move right
  • Move left
  • Jump
  • Throw bottle

About the Game

In "El Pollo Loco", you play as a brave hero fighting through a world filled with enemies. Your main objective is to defeat the evil Endboss and collect coins and bottles along the way. Use your skills to navigate through the levels, defeat enemies, and survive the challenges ahead!

Make sure to gather bottles to throw at enemies and collect coins to boost your score.
